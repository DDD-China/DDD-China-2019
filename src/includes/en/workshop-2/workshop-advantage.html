What You Can Gain

The essential concept of DDD: Beyond book knowledge, from a practical perspective, understand and master the core concepts and thinking framework of DDD, and the way of connecting them with Design Thinking.

Practical DDD strategy design approach: Analyze and abstract the business through the benchmark event storm workshop, unify the language, use the bounding context and the division of the problem domain to guide the alignment of the architecture design and business boundaries.

Practical DDD tactical design method: Through domain modeling, identify aggregates, entities, and value objects, visualize the design through C4 models and API documents while further refining the architecture design.

DDD technology implementation guiding ideology: introduces and shows you how to use the design guiding ideology of cloud native, micro-service and clean architecture to bring the design outcome of DDD to the final technical realization.
