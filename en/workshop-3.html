Workshop Introduction

Driven by the digital economy, all industries are facing a digital transformation. The IT system of the enterprise is transformed from supporting business to leading the development of the enterprise. Therefore, the enterprise architecture also needs new design ideas in the digital age. Platformization, sharization, and servicization etc. seem to have become a new direction for enterprise architecture. And how to split microservice in the process of service? What is the basis for microservice splitting? Is there any method or excellent practice as reference? How to ensure the correct landing of microservices? It is a problem that plagues many companies.
This workshop will use domain-driven design method and Event Storming practice to lead you to personally divide the micro-services in a sandbox business scenario, answer your confusion in the micro-service division, and lead you to experience the micro-service division into the process of development and deployment.

What You Can Gain

You'll learn how to use Domain Driven Design (DDD) to evolve business requirements into enterprise-level servicozed architecture design, then service development, and to experience the full domain-driven design (DDD) process. Let you master a set of excellent enterprise architecture design methods, which helps you have a nautical chart that can ride the wind and waves in the ever-changing digital age.
