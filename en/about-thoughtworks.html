About ThoughtWorks

ThoughtWorks is a leading global software and consulting company with 40 offices in 14 countries and over 6,000 industry elites serving global high-end customers through global resource sharing and talent exchange.

ThoughtWorks' business model is to achieve end-to-end digital services through strategic positioning, IT consulting, product design and software delivery, helping digital companies in the global financial, technology, manufacturing, retail and other industries to achieve digital transformation, the evolution of main corporate organization and technology architecture, and establish a digital strategic direction and enhance the digital capabilities of enterprises.

In China, ThoughtWorks has established six offices in Beijing, Xi'an, Chengdu, Wuhan, Shanghai and Shenzhen to provide world-class professional services to various enterprises, government departments and non-profit organizations. Using globally aligned consulting methods and technical capabilities, coupled with localized, customized service offerings, ThoughtWorks China serves local, forward-looking, high-end companies in the financial, insurance, communications, transportation and travel industries. Leading in the agile wave, they have helped a number of companies to optimize and transform their IT organizations, greatly improving the efficiency and creativity of their organizations.

Based on China's high-quality human resources, ThoughtWorks China has built a world-class software delivery center to provide high-end IT knowledge process outsourcing services to leading companies in various industries around the world. In Beijing, Xi'an and Chengdu, ThoughtWorks' software delivery center works in close cooperation with well-known foreign companies to solve problems with customer IT teams, deliver high-quality IT systems that are critical to the business, and help customers innovate and improve.

Original Intention of DDD China Conference

The DDD Conference was initiated by the architecture consultants of ThoughtWorks, a leader in domestic domain-driven design (DDD) thinking and practice. Hoping to provide a platform for domain-driven design (DDD) practitioners, in which they can interact and share successful experiences of their teams to each other, and enable Domain-Driven Design (DDD) architectural ideas to be recognized by more people in the country, which enlarges the scale of Domain-Driven Design (DDD) practices.
